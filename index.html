What I Believe

We keep moving forward, opening new doors, and doing new things, because we're curious and curiosity keeps leading us down new paths.

- Walt Disney

Observing

Observation is the first step in the scientific method. To observe means to notice the details. When something looks fuzzy I dive into the details to ask the questions that lead to new discoveries.

Questioning

It is through the process of questioning, seeking answers and asking further questions do I really learn. And sometimes questioning the assumptions can unravel great secrets.

Experimenting

"What gets measured gets done."

I believe experimentation is a great way to measure things to get enough information to make well-reasoned decisions.

My Career

My unique career pathway led me from a research lab studying fruit flies to court rooms litigating pharmaceutical cases to the streets of Accra consulting for micro-lending organizations.

A Career Unlike Any Other

I started out as a pre-med studying biological sciences. I didn't end up going to medical school, but I retained my passion for biotechnology and healthcare industry.

I used my knowledge of medical sciences to consult for law firms involved in pharmaceutical cases. There I began to appreciate the nuances of our legal system.

However, unsatisfied with my limited understanding of how decisions get made, I took a path that led me to business consulting.

Eventually, I realized that telling other people where to go is fun but helping them get there is more rewarding. And in the age of information, that means being able to properly utilize internet tools.

That's why I decided to jump into the world of web development to build stuff that makes things better.
